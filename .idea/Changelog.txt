Changelog v1.3.0 - "The Multiplier Update"
MAJOR CHANGES
- ALL tech boosts are now multipliers (% bonuses) and all previous issues with multipliers have been fixed
-- Existing savegames will receive the updated values without issue
-- New games will need to enable the `ai-blocked` script on game start
-- [Dev Note] Multipliers should improve mod balance *significantly*
BALANCING
- All Noble Competence boosts removed (they were only affecting AI), replaced with the following:
-- Garthimi Meme [THE HIVEMEND]: Stockpile job boost
-- Human Presence [HUMAN METROPOLIS]: Administration job boost
-- Human Focus [FLEXIBLE MINDS]: +1 Max Nobles (per rank)
-- Human Meme [AMBITION]: +3 Noble Ranks

Changelog v1.2.1
MAJOR CHANGES
- Monorace Focuses now have 6 ranks with effects that increase by 10% per rank (before they had 3 ranks and increased by 20% per rank)
-- [Dev Note] This should allow more wiggle room to decide how much you want to invest in the more extreme effects of these techs, and increases cost beyond Rank III
MINOR FIXES
- Garthimi Monorace Focus "HIVEMIND" now correctly reduces lifespan by 5 years per rank, instead of 0.2
- Removed the population requirement for Focuses and Memes, since it was redundant with the Presence tech requirement. No change on the player's side other than a cleaner display on tech info.
BALANCING
- Garthimi Monorace Focus "HIVEMIND" rebalanced across all effects
- Garthimi Meme "SACRIFICE" morale boost reduced, stamina boost added
- Amevian Monorace Focus "CULT OF THE DEPTHS" benefits have been reworked
- Cretonian Monorace Focus "STRONGER TOGETHER" now has a 3rd drawback, reduced work safety, to match balance spread of other monorace focuses, and increased immigration boost
- Dondorian Monorace Focus "BASTION OF ORDER" now has a 3rd drawback, increased trade tariff, and added increased judgement (desire for courts)
- Human Monorace Focus "STATE PATRIOTS" immigration drawback increased, loyalty boost reduced, and added increased punishment (desire for executions, etc.)
- Human Focus "FLEXIBLE MINDS" admin reduction replaced with morale reduction
- Human Focus "FREE WILL" loyalty reduction replaced with admin reduction

Changelog v1.2.0
MAJOR ADDITIONS
- Starting Bonus techs (the top-most tech in Culture trees) have been reworked:
-- Renamed to "Presence Bonus"
-- Every race has 3 Presence Bonus techs, requiring increasingly high pop counts to unlock (50 pops, 300 pops, 900 pops, respectively) 
-- Each race's Presence Bonus grants +15, +30, and +60 Gob points on unlock, respectively
-- Each Presence Bonus also grants a minor mostly-flavor stat bonus unique to that race (never more than 5%).
-- Each Presence Bonus has a flavorful name and lore blurb describing the race's presence and impact on your city.
-- New custom icons for every Presence Bonus.
-- [Dev Note] The purpose of this rework is to keep unlocks coming throughout the mid-game. Previously, you had to ignore Culture after spending your starting Gov points, until you unlocked Governor Nobles.
IMPROVEMENTS
- Improved and expanded Culture tech flavor text, adhering to lore based on the official lore primer doc.
-- Now over a hundred lines of flavor for the few of us that read that kind of stuff!
- Improved formatting of flavor text for all techs, so it stands out from the game-related tech text. 
NEXT UP
